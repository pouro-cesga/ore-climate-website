Welcome to the **Offshore renewable energy and climate (ORE-CLIMATE)** research group. 

We are dedicated to advancing the understanding of offshore renewable energy systems and their intersections with climate dynamics. Our research explores innovative technologies, marine environments, and sustainable energy solutions to combat climate change.

The group is lead by **Pablo Ouro** and co-lead by **Jose Carlos Fernandez**.

Explore our site to learn more about our projects, publications, and the amazing team driving our research forward.
